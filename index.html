ABOUT US

Burger

This mouthwatering juicy patty with the option of chicken or beef is a perfect meal for the whole family to enjoy. Our signature Burgers will fill you up!

Drinks

To salivate the warm throat with our delicious drink options! Cocktails, juices, smoothies, and even local favourites are among these delectable beverages.

Nachos

This is an excellent post-meal snack. Inspired by Mexican cuisine, we combined this menu with the Burger and Quesadillas. Both vegan and meat options are available.

Desserts

Sweet foods are typically served as part of the course. Our signature desserts are chocolate cake, tarts, and ice cream. Some dishes may contain coffee, cheeses, nuts, or other savoury items that are considered allergens by the customer.

Hot n' Spicy

Our spicy dishes are classified into three levels: Mild, Medium, and Hot. These are preferred by eaters who enjoy a challenge.

Chicken

Chicken is a great way to fill your stomach, and it tastes even better when combined with our Chicken Honey Quesadillas ©, Santa Chicken Quesadillas ©, and Ranch Chicken Quesadillas ©.
